Property Management Services

At Teske Realty Group, we offer comprehensive property management solutions designed to simplify ownership and maximize value for condo owners. Our flat-rate services ensure hassle-free management and fast rental results.

Flat-Rate Management - $100/Month

Our all-inclusive management service takes the stress out of property ownership, handling payments and maintenance with professionalism and efficiency.

  • Collect rent payments through our secure online payment portal on behalf of clients.
  • Address urgent maintenance requests promptly with our dedicated repair team.
  • Handle out-of-pocket repair costs by billing tenants directly or adding to the next month’s rent for client convenience.
